Post 8: The most significant difference between a recloser and a breaker is that the recloser was designed as a self-controlled device. Standards have been established and capabilities determined within the characteristics of the integral control scheme of the recloser.
Because a breaker was designed for use with a separate relay/control scheme, a breaker must be defined and rated for a wide variety of relay/control sequences.
